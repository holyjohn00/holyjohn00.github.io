The Role of AI

AI's role is to scale human effort in three critical areas:

  • **Personalization:** Tailoring millions of messages.
  • **Prediction:** Forecasting sales and customer churn.
  • **Automation:** Handling repetitive tasks instantly.

"75% of marketers now use AI for content creation, dramatically changing speed-to-market."

The Toolkit Strategy: The Rule of 3

The marketing technology landscape is crowded. Focus your learning on these three pillars to get hired.

Text & Content

The foundation: Writing copy, summarizing research, and generating SEO drafts.

Visual & Video

Driving engagement: Creating images, turning articles into video, and generating avatars.

Data & Prediction

The ROI: Understanding customer journey, sentiment, and forecasting campaign success.
